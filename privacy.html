Privacy Policy

Greenlight helps people share when they are available for a phone call.

Information We Use

Greenlight may ask for access to your contacts so you can organize people into groups and send availability signals to the right people.

Greenlight may use:

  • Contacts you choose to sync, including hashed phone numbers and email addresses used for matching
  • Availability windows you create
  • Groups you create
  • Optional notes you add to a greenlight
  • iCloud user record identifiers used to route greenlights
  • Device push notification tokens
  • Basic app diagnostics needed to keep the app working

Greenlight does not require a password or social login.

How We Use Information

We use information to let you create contact groups, show when people are available for a call, send and receive greenlight notifications, and maintain app reliability and support.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We only share information with service providers needed to operate the app, such as Apple iCloud and push notification services.

Contacts

Contacts are used to help you find and organize people you already know. Greenlight uses hashed contact handles for matching, so the app can identify which of your contacts are also using Greenlight without requiring a separate Greenlight account.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide Greenlight, support users, comply with law, and maintain security.

Your Choices

You can disable Contacts access or Notifications in iOS Settings at any time. You can also use Remove Cloud Sync in Greenlight Settings to remove local synced contacts and delete your Cloud matching handle from iCloud.
